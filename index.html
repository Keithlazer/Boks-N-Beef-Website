Our Heritage

Bridging superior cattle breeding with premium beef production

Founded in 2024, BoksNBeef combines generations of ranching expertise with modern meat science. We operate a vertically integrated operation where we control every aspect from breeding to processing.

Our breeding program focuses on developing cattle with superior genetics for both meat quality and robust health. We carefully select bulls and heifers based on performance data, EPDs, and conformation.

For our beef products, we implement a 28-45 day dry-aging process that enhances flavor and tenderness, ensuring only the highest quality reaches your table.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our cattle and beef products

We specialize in Brahman, Angus, and Hereford breeds. Our Brahman cattle excel in heat tolerance and adaptability, while our Angus provide superior marbling and meat quality. Hereford cattle offer excellent feed efficiency and hardiness.

Our beef is dry-aged for 28-45 days in our climate-controlled aging room. This process enhances flavor, tenderness, and creates a more concentrated beef taste. Premium cuts like ribeye receive extended aging for maximum flavor development.

Yes, we provide delivery services for both live cattle and packaged beef products. We serve the greater Harare area and surrounding regions. Delivery fees vary based on distance and order size. Contact us for specific pricing.

Our breeding program focuses on genetic excellence and performance. We use artificial insemination with semen from top-performing bulls, maintain detailed EPD records, and select for traits like growth rate, marbling, and maternal qualities.

Quality starts with our breeding program and continues through careful feeding, humane handling, and precise processing. We follow strict protocols for food safety and quality control, with regular testing and monitoring throughout the process.

Yes, we welcome visitors by appointment. Ranch tours are available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 4 PM. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance to schedule your visit and ensure we can provide you with the best experience.
